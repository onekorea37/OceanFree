Ocean Free

Help to clean the ocean

Do you love the ocean
and want to protect it from pollution?



Then this game is for you!



Ocean free is a Unity-based video game designed
to raise awareness about the importance of keeping our oceans clean
In this game, your task is to prevent trash from by clicking on it as soon as it appears.

The more trash you click, the more points you earn!

But, be careful, some trash items are faster and trickier than others.
As you progress through the game, the level of difficulty increases,
and you will encounter different types of trash,
such as plastic bottles, fishing nets, and even toxic waste.

The game is designed to educate players about the ecological crises that are happening around the world and how your actions can make a difference in protecting the environment.
Start playing "Ocean Free" today and see how many points you can score while learning about the importance of environmental conservation.
